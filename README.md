
# ADHCRI

## ASEAN Digital Health & Climate Resilience Intelligence Platform

### Problem Statement

ASEAN healthcare systems face fragmented data ecosystems, unequal healthcare capacity, communicable disease burdens, and climate-related health risks, limiting timely decision-making and regional coordination.

### Solution

ADHCRI is an AI-powered regional health intelligence platform that integrates:

- Disease Surveillance
- Maternal & Child Health Monitoring
- Workforce Analytics
- Climate Intelligence
- Healthcare Investment Analytics
- Cross-Border Data Sharing

### Key Benefits

- Early Outbreak Detection
- Predictive Analytics
- Resource Optimization
- Healthcare Workforce Planning
- Health Equity Monitoring
- Regional Resilience

### Deliverables

- Business Requirements Document (BRD)
- Executive Dashboard
- Cost-Benefit Analysis
- Gap Analysis
- Regional Readiness Assessment
- Executive Presentation
- Video Storyboard

### SDGs Supported

- SDG 3: Good Health & Well-Being
- SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities
- SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

### Project Tagline

Transforming Health Data into Actionable Intelligence for a Resilient ASEAN.
